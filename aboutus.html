About Us

Overview With cool summers and snowy winters, the Happy Cabin offers a beautiful and serene setting all year long. The Happy Cabin is just minutes away from hiking and golf in the summer and Sunrise ski resort in the winter. Use our bikes, ski, relax on our deck, golf, hike, horseback ride, ski, snowboard, mountain bike, fish, eat, see a movie, or anything else... The Happy Cabin is just minutes away from everything. The cabin sleeps 8, is dog friendly, and is located in the White Mountains of Pinetop, AZ.

We would love for you to be our guest.
