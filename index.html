The Happy Cabin is a very cozy 1850 sq. ft wood cabin with great installation to keep you warm in the winters. It sleeps up to 7 people with 3 bed rooms and 2 bathrooms. Inside the cabin there are plenty of games and activities for the kids. The yard has lots of room as well for a variety of activities. Care for a game of badminton? The location is great, if you just wanted to enjoy a nice walk around the neighborhood and the house is only a short drive to the local restaurants/stores. Come enjoy and have a fun stay at the Happy Cabin!
